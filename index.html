Cargill Risk Management

Client: Cargill, MN
Role: Front End UI Developer
Team Size: 5
Period: Jul 2019 - Present
Domain: Trading
PROJECT DESCRIPTION

CRM is a risk management application suite for the Cargill trading domain. These are web applications used by customers and traders to quote, submit orders, create trades, and manage positions for CRM end users (viz., Energy and Agriculture).

Customers can view up-to-date information about the value of their portfolio of trades that are currently active with CRM, cash and credit details and receive trade recap emails on a regular basis.
